Toast

Description:

A very complicated toast recipe.

Utensils:

  • Toaster
  • Plate

Ingredients (for X Slices of Toast):

  • X slices of non-toasted bread

Steps:

  1. Placing as many slices of bread into the toaster as can fit without having to squash multiple slices together.
  2. Turn on the toaster and uses its default timing; if the bread starts smoking however, turn it off early.
  3. Once the bread is toasted, place it on the plate.
  4. Repeat all the above steps for all the non-toasted slices of bread.
