Special Fried Rice

Description:

Sacrilege of a Chinese recipe I found in Fuschia Dunlop's Every Grain of Rice: Simple Chinese Home Cooking.

This recipe is quick and easy and uses few ingredients.

Ingredients (2 Meals):

  • 1 large onion
  • 1 large tomato
  • 2 spring onions
  • 2 medium-sized eggs
  • 150g cooked rice (that has been refrigerated overnight or is in a microwaveable rice pouch)
  • Olive oil
  • Soy sauce
  • Black pepper
  • Sesame oil

Steps:

  1. Cut one of the onions in half.
  2. Take one half of the onion and slice it into strips from above.
  3. Repeat the above step with the other half of the onion.
  4. Repeat all above steps with the other onion.
  5. Repeat all above steps with each of the tomatoes.
  6. Slice the spring onions into short cylinders, grouping together the green-leafy parts and the white-onion parts.
  7. Crack the eggs and empty their contents into the same bowl.
  8. With the egg whisk, beat the contents of the bowl until consistent mixed thoroughly.
  9. Add 2 tsp of olive oil to a wok and raise it to medium heat.
  10. When you can feel heat radiating above the wok, pour in the egg mixture. When its edges have begun to solidify, using the wooden spoon, draw from the edge of the egg disc to its centre, allowing mixture that hasn't touched the wok to set too; do the same from the other side of the egg disk, too.
  11. Once most of the egg has begun to set, use the wooden spoon to roughly cut the egg into bite-size pieces, stir the egg until no more liquid mixture can be seen, then empty the egg back into the bowl.
  12. Add another 2 tsp of olive oil to the wok followed by the onions and shift them around with the wooden spoon until softened and lightly browned--should take 3-5 minutes.
  13. Now, add the tomatoes, egg and white-onion parts of the spring onions and continue to stir for another 2 minutes.
  14. Raise heat to medium-high, pour in the rice and stir until it is hot.
  15. Stir in a drizzle of soy sauce and 1 tsp of sesame oil.
  16. Serve immediately with the green-leafy parts of the spring onions and some black pepper on top and enjoy!
